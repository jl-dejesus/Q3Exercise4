Pico de Loro


Pico de Loro is the first beach I have ever been to. It was an exciting experience and a trip I looked foward to. My family, along with my aunt's family, took a four hour drive from Manila to Batangas. As soon as we got there, we immediately went to the beach to feel the cool sea breeze and to walk along the grainy white sand. The air was fresh and the view was mesmerizing.

The following day, my uncle brought my cousins and I out to the coast. He got us a 30-minute ride on a jetski! My cousin drove while I sat at the back. It was a little scary at first, but eventually the waves of the sea calmed my nerves. The waves were a little too strong though. At one point, the waves were too strong that it tipped the jetski over, and my cousin and I fell into the sea. Thank goodness for our life vests, we were able to stay afloat and quickly climb back onto the jetski. We spent the afternoon playing badminton at the Country Club, and ended the day with bowling and snacks.

On our third and last day, my entire family went to the beach to take one last dip. We tried to build sandcastles, but sadly, they kept sticking to the mold :( It's okay though! I was able to ride the jetski there one last time, this time by myself. After our short trip to the beach, we went back to our room, packed our things, and said goodbye.

I was so sad when we left, the time felt so short, and I wanted to visit more sights. I am more happy though. I finally made it to the beach! I hope I'll be able to visit other beaches in the future soon. :D
